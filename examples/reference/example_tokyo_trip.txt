## Top Tips
1. Get JR Pass before arrival - saves SO much money
2. Convenience stores are lifesavers (cheap, good food)
3. Visit popular spots early morning or late evening
4. Pocket WiFi was essential ($8/day)
5. IC card (Suica/Pasmo) makes transport super easy
6. Most places don't take credit cards - bring cash!
7. Download Google Maps offline for Tokyo
